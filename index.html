Partner Universities

Rostov State Medical University

Rostov State Medical University, founded in 1930 on the basis of the medical faculty of the Warsaw Russian University, is located in the picturesque center of the city. Its campus includes academic departments, clinics, a sports complex, student dormitories, a canteen and a student polyclinic.

Don State Technical University

Don State Technical University was established on May 20, 1930. Aleading russian university in the field of engineering

A leading russian university estblished in 1915. A leading position in the area of innovations and research; integration of research into education; a winning combination of fundamental and applied research; highly reputed schools of thought; open-minded and creative teaching staff; up-to-date research facilities and appliances; collaboration with the Russian Academy of Sciences and international research facilities
